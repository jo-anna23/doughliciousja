# Jo-Anna Martinez | doughliciousja

Doughlicious JA is a dynamic e‑commerce website built for the Web Programming course (Year 3, Semester 2) at the University of Technology, Jamaica (UTech).
This project represents a for‑profit business concept — an online bakery — showcasing my ability to design and develop a responsive, user‑friendly site using vanilla HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

## Business Concept
• Mission: To deliver exceptional, handcrafted baked goods that brings sweetness and joy to every celebration - making custom cake ordering simple, fun and delicious for every Jamaican.
• Vision: To become Jamaica's most beloved bakery, known for quality, creativity and making every occasion unforgettable - one cake at a time.
• Slogan: “Baked with Love, Delivered with Joy.”
• Target Audience: Individuals & Families (in Jamaica) who want easy access to baked goods without leaving home  as well as Event Planners & Corporate Clients who prefer to place custom orders with specific design requirements & guaranteed delivery.

## Sections
• Home – Hero introduction and featured bakery items
• Products – Browse baked goods with images and descriptions
• Cart – Add, view, and manage items with dynamic totals (subtotal, discount, tax, total)
• Checkout – Enter shipping and payment details, confirm or cancel orders
• Login & Registration – Secure forms with JavaScript validation (username, password, email, DOB)
• About Us – Business details, student information, and contact link via mailto

## Technologies Used
• HTML5 – Semantic structure and clean markup
• CSS3 – Custom properties, Flexbox, CSS Grid, responsive media queries, consistent color palette
• JavaScript (ES6) – DOM manipulation, event handling, form validation, cart logic
• Google Fonts – Friendly, modern typography
• GitHub Pages – Free hosting for live deployment
